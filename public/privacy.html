Privacy Policy for Bantay Barangay

Last updated: November 7, 2025

Information We Collect

When you use Bantay Barangay Messenger bot, we collect:

  • Your Facebook Messenger ID (for responding to messages)
  • Incident reports you submit (description, location, category)
  • Message timestamps

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and respond to incident reports
  • Provide community safety updates
  • Improve our emergency response services

Information Sharing

We may share incident reports with:

  • Local Barangay officials for emergency response
  • Emergency services when necessary

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact your Barangay office or the bot administrator.
